# Full Implementation Rollout

## English
**Subject:** Full Rollout of SMED and TPM (6S Project)

**Dear All Staff,**

Following our successful pilot, we are now rolling out the SMED and Total Productive Maintenance (TPM) procedures across all production lines.

The pilot showed a 40% reduction in changeover times and a significant drop in unplanned downtime. Training sessions for all operators will begin next week.

Please review the attached schedule to find your designated training slot.
