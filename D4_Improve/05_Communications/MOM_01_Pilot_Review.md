# Minutes of Meeting — Pilot Review

## English
**Meeting Date:** August 15, 2026
**Time:** 11:00 AM - 12:00 PM
**Location:** Main Conference Room — Building A
**Attendees:** Meshary (Project Lead), Ahmed Al-Fahad (Process Owner), Maintenance Lead, QC Lead

### Agenda:
1. Review of Smart Sensor Pilot Results
2. Review of SMED implementation on Line 1
3. Go/No-Go decision for full rollout

### Key Discussion Points:
- The new torque sensors on Line 1 reduced capping defects from 4.5% to 1.2% during the 2-week pilot.
- The new SMED procedure, including shadow boards for tools, reduced changeover time from 45 minutes to 22 minutes.
- Operators reported high satisfaction with the shadow boards, noting it significantly reduced stress during changeovers.
- Maintenance Lead confirmed that the new sensors are easy to calibrate and integrate well with the existing PLC.

### Action Items:
- **Meshary:** Prepare the Full Rollout communication plan by August 17.
- **Maintenance Lead:** Schedule installation of new sensors on Lines 2 and 3 for the upcoming weekend.
- **Ahmed Al-Fahad:** Approve the purchase order for additional shadow boards for all lines.

- أكد رئيس قسم الصيانة أن المستشعرات الجديدة سهلة المعايرة وتتكامل بشكل جيد مع وحدة التحكم المنطقية القابلة للبرمجة الحالية.
