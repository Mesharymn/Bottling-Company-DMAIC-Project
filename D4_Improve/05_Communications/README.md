# 05 — Communications
## Water Bottling Company | Improve Phase

> **Purpose:** To keep all stakeholders aligned, informed, and engaged during the critical deployment of solutions.

---

## Communication Plan

This folder contains all official project communications for the Improve phase. 

| Stakeholder | Frequency | Channel | Content |
|-------------|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Plant Manager** | Weekly | Email Update | Gantt progress, Pilot results, ROI tracking |
| **Maintenance Lead** | Daily | Stand-up | TPM rollout status, Sensor installation blockers |
| **Production Supervisors** | Daily | Kaizen Huddle | Kanban card issues, SMED training progress |
| **Operators** | Pre-Shift | Visual Board | "What's changing today" + Success stories |

---

## Email Templates

- `EMAIL_01_Pilot_Launch.md`: Sent to production team before the 500ml-1.5L SMED pilot.
- `EMAIL_02_Pilot_Results.md`: Sent to management with statistical validation of the pilot.
- `EMAIL_03_Full_Rollout.md`: Sent plant-wide before full deployment of Kanban and TPM.

*(Templates will be populated as the phase progresses).*
