# 05 — Communications
## Water Bottling Company | Control Phase

> **Purpose:** To officially close the project, celebrate the team's success, and communicate the sustained results to the wider organization.

---

## Communication Plan

This folder contains all official project closure communications.

| Stakeholder | Frequency | Channel | Content |
|-------------|-----------|---------|---------|
| **Executive Sponsors** | Once | Final Report | Verified ROI, before/after metrics, lessons learned |
| **Plant Manager** | Once | Handover Meeting | Control Plan sign-off, SOP transfer |
| **Production Team** | Once | Town Hall / Celebration | Recognizing key contributors, final results |

---

## Email Templates

- `EMAIL_01_Project_Closure.md`: Sent plant-wide announcing the successful conclusion of the DMAIC project and highlighting the final metrics.
- `EMAIL_02_Handover_Signoff.md`: Sent to the Process Owner with all links to Control Plan and SOPs.

*(Templates will be populated as the phase concludes).*
