# Stakeholder Mapping

## Power / Interest / Impact

| STAKEHOLDER | POWER (H/M/L) | INTEREST (H/M/L) | IMPACT (H/M/L) | ATTITUDE (SUPPORT/NEUTRAL/RESIST) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Operations Management | H | H | H | Support |
| Production Operators | M | M | H | Neutral (Potential Resist if changes are complex) |
| Maintenance Team | M | H | H | Support (Reduces their reactive workload) |

## Stakeholder vs CTQ

| STAKEHOLDER | WHAT THEY CARE ABOUT = CTQ | CONFLICT WITH OTHERS ? |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Operations Management | Line output (22k/hr), Downtime (≤10%) | Push for speed may conflict with QC's focus on defect rates |
| Production Operators | Machine uptime, smooth changeovers | May resist new procedures from Maintenance/Management |
| Maintenance Team | Changeover time (≤20 min), Machine uptime | Preventive maintenance time conflicts with Planning's schedule |
| Quality Control Team | Defect rate (≤1%), Rework rate (≤2%) | Stopping line for quality conflicts with Operators' output goals |
| Supply Chain / Planning Team | On-time order fulfillment (≥95%), Inventory balance | Scheduling short runs conflicts with Maintenance changeover times |
| Distributors / Retailers | Defect rate (≤1%), On-time order fulfillment (≥95%) | None directly, but they apply pressure on Planning |
