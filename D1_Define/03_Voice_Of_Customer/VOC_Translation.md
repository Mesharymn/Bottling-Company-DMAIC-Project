# Voice of Customer (VOC) Translation

| SOURCE | RAW VOC | WHY ? (Underlying Issue) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| External (Retailer) | "Some bottles are leaking" | Defective capping/sealing process; poor quality control |
| External (Distributor) | "Packaging quality is inconsistent" | Shrink wrap or carton machinery is malfunctioning or out of alignment |
| External (Retailer) | "Orders are not delivered on time" | Production downtime and slow changeovers delay dispatch |
| Internal (Sales) | "We don’t always have stock when needed" | Production is unreliable; inventory imbalance (wrong SKUs produced) |
| External (Retailer) | "We don’t always get the quantities we ordered" | High rejection/rework rates reduce final yield; stockouts |
| External (Distributor) | "Product quality is not consistent across batches" | Process variation between shifts; lack of standardized settings |
| External (Retailer) | "Labels are sometimes misaligned or damaged" | Labeling machine calibration issues; frequent minor stoppages |
| Internal (Sales) | "Production is unreliable" | High downtime (18%) and long changeovers (45 min) disrupt schedules |
