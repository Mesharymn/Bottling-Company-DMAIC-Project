# Project Charter

**Project Title:** Production Line Efficiency and Quality Improvement

## Business Case
*(Why this matters, What is the problem? What is the impact (financial/operational)?)*

The Water Bottling Company is experiencing significant production downtime (18%) and high defect rates (4.5%), leading to a 35% increase in customer complaints and a drop in on-time order fulfillment to 82%. Operationally, this causes severe inventory imbalances, frequent rework (6%), and long changeover times (45 min). Financially, the company faces delayed payments, returned batches, and potential loss of key retail distributors to competitors. Addressing these issues is critical to stabilizing supply, restoring customer trust, and recovering lost margins.

## Problem Statement (Data-based)
**Format:** Current performance is ___ compared to target ___, resulting in ___ impact.

Current production output is **18,000 bottles/hour** compared to a target of **22,000 bottles/hour**, and the defect rate is **4.5%** compared to a target of **≤ 1%**. This results in an **18% failure to fulfill orders on time** and a **35% increase in customer complaints**.

## Goal Statement (SMART)
**Format:** Reduce/Increase ___ from ___ to ___ within ___ timeframe.

* **Increase** line output from 18,000 to 22,000 bottles/hour within 6 months.
* **Reduce** downtime from 18% to ≤ 10% within 6 months.
* **Reduce** defect rate from 4.5% to ≤ 1% within 6 months.
* **Reduce** changeover time from 45 min to ≤ 20 min within 6 months.

## People

| Name | Role | Tasks |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Operations Manager | Sponsor / Champion | Approve resources, remove roadblocks, oversee overall plant performance |
| Production Supervisor | Process Owner | Execute daily production, manage line operators, implement changes |
| Quality Control Lead | SME (Quality) | Monitor defect rates, validate sealing/packaging standards |
| Maintenance Lead | SME (Equipment) | Reduce minor stoppages, optimize changeover procedures |
| Supply Chain Planner | Team Member | Balance inventory, schedule production runs to minimize changeovers |

## Scope

| In Scope | Out of Scope |
| :--- | :--- |
| Bottling line operations (blowing, filling, capping, labeling, packaging) | Water sourcing and initial raw water transport |
| Changeover procedures between SKUs (330ml, 500ml, 1.5L) | Marketing and external sales strategies |
| Quality control checks on the production line | Vehicle fleet maintenance for distribution |
| Maintenance schedules and minor stoppage reduction | Redesigning the physical bottle shapes |
| Inventory balancing for finished goods | Financial accounting and payroll |
