# Minutes of Meeting — Root Cause Analysis Workshop

## English
**Meeting Date:** July 15, 2026
**Time:** 10:00 AM - 12:30 PM
**Location:** Main Conference Room — Building A
**Attendees:** Meshary (Project Lead), Ahmed Al-Fahad (Process Owner), Maintenance Lead, QC Lead, 2 Line Operators

### Agenda:
1. Review of Measure phase data (Downtime & Defects)
2. Fishbone (Ishikawa) Diagram creation
3. 5 Why's Analysis on top 3 issues
4. Prioritization of root causes

### Key Discussion Points:
- The team reviewed the Pareto chart, which showed that 80% of capping defects come from Head #3 on the Capping Machine.
- Through 5 Why's, the Maintenance Lead identified that the torque sensor on Head #3 is worn out and hasn't been replaced due to a lack of spare parts in inventory.
- For changeover delays, operators noted that tools are often missing or shared between lines, leading to a 15-minute search time per changeover.
- The lack of a standardized SMED (Single-Minute Exchange of Die) procedure was confirmed as a primary cause for extended changeover times.

### Action Items:
- **Maintenance Lead:** Order 5 new torque sensors immediately (by July 17).
- **Meshary:** Draft the initial SMED observation plan by July 20.
- **QC Lead:** Monitor Head #3 specifically over the next week to gather more granular defect data.

### بنود العمل:
