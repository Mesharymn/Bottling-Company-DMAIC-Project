# Minutes of Meeting — Measurement System Analysis (MSA)

## English
**Meeting Date:** July 25, 2026
**Time:** 02:00 PM - 03:00 PM
**Location:** Quality Control Lab
**Attendees:** Meshary (Project Lead), QC Lead, 3 QC Inspectors

### Agenda:
1. Review of current defect measurement process
2. Plan for Gage R&R Study (Capping Defects)
3. Standardization of visual inspection criteria

### Key Discussion Points:
- The team identified that "poor sealing" is often subjectively evaluated by different inspectors.
- Agreed to conduct an Attribute Agreement Analysis (Gage R&R) using 50 sample bottles (30 good, 20 defective) evaluated by 3 inspectors across 2 trials.
- QC Lead requested a physical "Defect Boundary Board" to standardize visual checks on the line.

### Action Items:
- **QC Lead:** Prepare the 50 sample bottles for the Gage R&R study by July 27.
- **Meshary:** Facilitate the Gage R&R study and analyze results in Minitab by July 30.
- **Meshary:** Draft the initial Defect Boundary Board based on historical samples by August 5.
